ABOUT US

MAD JAM FEST is an event that brings together multiple creative categories. MAD Game Jam, MAD Photo Jam, MAD Video Jam and MAD Animation Jam.

If you are a video game enthusiast looking for new projects, an animator seeking new challenges, a photographer or filmmaker, or simply curious about audiovisual creation — this event is for you.
