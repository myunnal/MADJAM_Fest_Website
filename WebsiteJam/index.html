ABOUT US

MAD JAM FEST is an event that brings together multiple incredible creative experiences: MAD Game Jam, MAD Photo Jam, MAD Video Jam, MAD Motion Jam. MAD Jam Fest is a 48-hour intensive creative space that challenges artistic production and serves as a laboratory for experimentation and performance in the areas of video games, animation, photography, videography, and cinematography.


If you are a video game enthusiast looking for new creations, an animator seeking to experiment with new forms of expression, a photographer or filmmaker in search of new horizons, or simply someone who is curious and ready for new challenges exploring unique and experimental approaches to video, video games, or animation, MAD Jam Fest is for you.

The MAD Game Jam competition challenges teams to create video games in 48 hours, based on a theme revealed on the first day of the event. It is an intense space for creation that seeks new, creative approaches to the world of computer games through the intersection of different areas of knowledge.

The MAD Photo Jam challenges participants to create an original photographic project in an intensive 48-hour format, based on a theme revealed on the first day of the event. Participants must be fully autonomous in providing the equipment needed to develop their project.

Open call for intensive artistic creations in motion design and animation, combining diverse techniques to respond to a theme revealed on the first day of the event. These techniques may include both 2D and 3D motion and animation, giving participants the opportunity to explore exclusively digital content over a 48-hour period.

The MAD Video Jam competition challenges teams to create audiovisual projects within 48 hours on a theme revealed at the event's start. It promotes a creative approach to developing short films, documentaries, ads, experimental videos, music videos, and more.
