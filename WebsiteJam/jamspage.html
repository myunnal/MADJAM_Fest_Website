GAME

The MAD Game Jam competition challenges several teams to create, in 48 hours in a row, video games on all possible platforms and in all possible formats of the event. This competition takes place last weekend in February and promotes a comprehensive and intensive creative that acts in the interactions between areas of knowledge new creative approaches to the universe of computer games.

Rules:

  • The teams develop the games in 48 hours, on a theme that they will only know on the first day.
  • The papers are presented to a panel of juries after about 48 hours.
  • The jury can be made up alone or in teams of 4-5 members, presenting the areas they want to explore.

The presentation of the works after 48 hours will be assigned and subject to evaluation, where the 3 best teams will be selected and awarded with prizes. It will be awarded 4 honorable mentions for the rewarded teams.

VIDEO

The MAD Video Jam competition challenges participants to create compelling video content within 48 hours. This intensive creative process encourages innovative storytelling, cinematography, and editing techniques across various video formats and genres.

Rules:

  • Participants create videos in 48 hours on a theme revealed at the start.
  • Final works are presented to a jury panel for evaluation.
  • Teams can work solo or in groups of 4-5 members.

Winners will be selected based on creativity, technical execution, and adherence to the theme, with prizes awarded to top entries.

PHOTO

The MAD Photo Jam challenges photographers to capture and curate a stunning photo series within 48 hours. Participants explore creative perspectives, lighting, composition, and post-processing to create a cohesive visual narrative.

Rules:

  • Create a photo series in 48 hours based on the announced theme.
  • Present your work to an expert jury for evaluation.
  • Work individually or in small teams.

Top submissions will receive recognition and prizes based on artistic merit and technical quality.

ANIMATION

The MAD Animation Jam invites animators to bring stories to life through motion within 48 hours. Whether 2D, 3D, stop-motion, or experimental, this jam celebrates the art of animation in all its forms.

Rules:

  • Develop animations in 48 hours following the revealed theme.
  • Submit your work for jury evaluation.
  • Collaborate in teams or work solo.

Awards will be given to the most creative and technically impressive animations.
